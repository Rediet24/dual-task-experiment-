Dual Task Experiment 

	This study examines your ability to multi-task.  You will simultaneously complete two different tasks.  Both require visual attention.  

	The main task (primary task) is to track the dot in the box.  Use your mouse to do this.  When you are on target, the box will turn blue.  

	The other task (secondary task) is to pay attention to the letters in the center of the screen.  When an "X" appears, click on the space bar. 
	Approximately half of the letters that will be presented are "X".
